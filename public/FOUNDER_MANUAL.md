## 1. Getting Started

### Overview of the Platform
Skyymarkai is a next-generation platform designed to empower founders with the tools, automation, and intelligence needed to scale modern businesses. As a founder, you have access to a robust suite of features for managing agents, automating workflows, and maintaining full control over your workspace. The platform is built with security, flexibility, and growth in mind, ensuring you can adapt to changing business needs while keeping your data and operations secure.

Skyymarkai’s dashboard is your command center. Here, you can view real-time analytics, manage your team, configure agents, and access all administrative controls. The intuitive interface is designed to minimize friction, so you can focus on strategic decisions rather than technical hurdles. Whether you’re onboarding your first team member or scaling to hundreds, Skyymarkai grows with you.

### Initial Setup Steps
Getting started is simple, but a thoughtful setup ensures you maximize the platform’s potential:

1. **Sign in with your founder email:** Use your designated founder email (such as cambrianenergy@gmail.com or financialgrowthdfw@gmail.com) to unlock full access. This ensures you receive founder-only privileges and can access confidential resources.
2. **Create or select a workspace:** Workspaces are containers for your projects, teams, and data. You can create multiple workspaces for different business units or initiatives. Each workspace is isolated for security and management.
3. **Invite team members and assign roles:** Add your core team, assigning roles such as owner, admin, or member. Role-based access ensures sensitive features remain protected while empowering your team to contribute effectively.
4. **Add agents and templates:** Agents are the backbone of automation in Skyymarkai. Add agents tailored to your business needs, and use templates to standardize workflows. Templates can be customized and shared across workspaces.
5. **Configure billing and integrations:** Set up your billing information and connect integrations like Stripe for seamless payment processing. Review your plan tier and upgrade as needed to unlock additional features.

### Navigating the Dashboard
The dashboard is organized for clarity and speed:

- **Sidebar Navigation:** Quickly access billing, agents, templates, analytics, and the founder manual. The sidebar remains visible for easy switching between sections.
- **Workspace Switcher:** Click your workspace name at the top to switch between different workspaces. Each workspace maintains its own settings, agents, and data.
- **Notifications & Alerts:** Stay informed about important updates, billing reminders, and agent activity through the notification center.
- **Search & Shortcuts:** Use the global search to find agents, templates, or documentation instantly. Keyboard shortcuts are available for power users.

### Key Resources for Founders
As a founder, you have access to exclusive resources:

- **Support Portal:** [support.skyymarkai.com](https://support.skyymarkai.com) — Access live chat, submit tickets, and browse the knowledge base.
- **API Documentation:** [docs.skyymarkai.com/api](https://docs.skyymarkai.com/api) — Integrate Skyymarkai with your existing tools and automate advanced workflows.
- **Community Forum:** [community.skyymarkai.com](https://community.skyymarkai.com) — Connect with other founders, share best practices, and get feedback on your strategies.
- **Founder Manual:** This manual is always available from the dashboard for quick reference. Use the clickable table of contents to jump to any section.

#### Pro Tips
- Bookmark the dashboard for one-click access.
- Set up two-factor authentication immediately for enhanced security.
- Schedule a 1:1 onboarding session with Skyymarkai support for personalized guidance.

## 3. Agent Management

### Adding New Agents to Your Workspace
Agents are the core automation units in Skyymarkai. As a founder, you can add new agents to your workspace at any time. To do so:
1. Navigate to the Agents section in your dashboard.
2. Click "Add Agent" and select from a library of pre-built agent types or start from scratch.
3. Configure the agent’s name, description, and core duties. Assign it to a relevant workspace or project.
4. Set permissions and access levels for the agent, ensuring it only interacts with the data and workflows you specify.

#### Tips:
- Start with a pilot agent to test new workflows before scaling.
- Use agent templates for rapid deployment of common automation patterns.

### Assigning Agents to Workflows
Workflows are sequences of tasks that agents execute. Assigning agents to workflows allows you to automate complex business processes:
- Drag and drop agents into workflow diagrams using the visual editor.
- Define triggers, conditions, and actions for each agent within the workflow.
- Set up notifications for workflow completion, errors, or manual intervention points.

#### Example:
Assign a "Follow-Up Sequencer" agent to automatically send emails to leads after a sales call, escalating to a human if no response is received.

### Monitoring Agent Performance
Continuous monitoring ensures your agents are operating efficiently:
- Access real-time analytics and logs for each agent.
- Set up performance dashboards to track key metrics such as task completion rate, error frequency, and response times.
- Receive alerts for abnormal behavior or failures.

#### Best Practices:
- Schedule regular reviews of agent performance data.
- Use analytics to identify bottlenecks and optimize workflows.

### Customizing Agent Duties and Permissions
Every agent can be tailored to your business needs:
- Edit agent duties to add, remove, or modify tasks.
- Adjust permissions to restrict access to sensitive data or actions.
- Use role-based controls to ensure only authorized users can modify agent settings.

#### Security Note:
Always review permissions when onboarding new agents or changing workflow structures.

### Troubleshooting Agent Issues
If an agent encounters errors or unexpected behavior:
- Check the agent’s log files for detailed error messages.
- Use the built-in troubleshooting wizard to diagnose common problems.
- Restart or reset agents as needed.
- Contact Skyymarkai support for advanced troubleshooting or bug reports.

## 4. Workspace Administration

### Creating and Managing Workspaces
Workspaces are the organizational backbone of Skyymarkai. Each workspace is a secure, isolated environment for your projects, teams, and data. As a founder, you can create multiple workspaces to separate business units, clients, or initiatives.

#### How to Create a Workspace:
1. Click the "Create Workspace" button in your dashboard.
2. Enter a unique name and description for the workspace.
3. Configure default settings, such as region, data retention, and access controls.
4. Invite initial team members and assign roles.

#### Managing Workspaces:
- Switch between workspaces using the workspace switcher at the top of the dashboard.
- Archive or delete workspaces that are no longer needed (note: deletion is permanent and requires confirmation).
- Review workspace activity logs for security and compliance.

### Inviting and Removing Team Members
Collaboration is key to scaling your business. As a founder, you can invite new members and manage existing ones:
- Send invitations via email, specifying the role (owner, admin, member).
- Remove inactive or unauthorized users to maintain security.
- Use bulk actions to manage large teams efficiently.

#### Best Practices:
- Regularly audit team membership and roles.
- Require two-factor authentication for all admins and owners.

### Setting Workspace Roles (Owner, Admin, Member)
Roles determine what actions a user can perform within a workspace:
- **Owner:** Full control, including billing, agent management, and workspace settings.
- **Admin:** Can manage agents, templates, and workflows, but cannot access billing.
- **Member:** Can participate in workflows and use agents, but cannot modify settings.

#### Role Management Tips:
- Assign the minimum necessary permissions to each user.
- Review and update roles as your team grows or changes.

### Workspace Metadata and Settings
Each workspace has customizable metadata and settings:
- Set workspace goals, tags, and descriptions for clarity.
- Configure notification preferences and integrations.
- Manage data retention policies and compliance settings.

### Data Backup and Recovery
Protecting your data is critical:
- Schedule automatic backups of all workspace data.
- Download manual backups before making major changes.
- Use the recovery wizard to restore data in case of accidental deletion or corruption.

## 5. Security & Access Control

### Protecting Sensitive Data
Data security is a top priority for founders. Skyymarkai employs industry-standard encryption, both in transit and at rest, to safeguard your information. As a founder, you are responsible for enforcing best practices within your team:
- Limit access to sensitive data using role-based permissions.
- Regularly review access logs and audit trails.
- Use secure, unique passwords for all accounts.
- Educate your team on phishing and social engineering threats.

#### Data Handling Tips:
- Never share API keys or credentials in public channels.
- Use environment variables for sensitive configuration.

### Role-Based Access Management
Assigning the right roles to team members is crucial for security and efficiency:
- Use the admin console to assign roles (founder, owner, admin, member).
- Restrict high-risk actions (like billing or agent deletion) to founders and owners only.
- Review role assignments quarterly or after major team changes.

### Enforcing Founder-Only Controls
Certain features and documentation are restricted to founders:
- The founder manual and advanced settings are only visible to founder accounts.
- Use audit logs to verify that only authorized users access sensitive features.
- Set up alerts for unauthorized access attempts.

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Setup
Enhance security by enabling 2FA for all high-privilege accounts:
- Go to your account settings and enable 2FA using an authenticator app or SMS.
- Require 2FA for all admins and owners.
- Periodically test 2FA recovery procedures.

### Security Best Practices
- Rotate passwords and API keys regularly.
- Enable session timeouts for inactive users.
- Monitor for unusual login activity and respond promptly to alerts.

#### Incident Response:
If you suspect a security breach, immediately revoke affected credentials, notify your team, and contact Skyymarkai support for assistance.

### Managing Payment Methods
Add, update, or remove payment methods securely:

- Set a default payment method for recurring charges.
- Receive notifications for expiring cards or failed payments.

## 7. Stripe Integration

### Connecting Stripe to Your Workspace
Stripe is the backbone of Skyymarkai’s payment processing. As a founder, you can connect your workspace to Stripe for seamless billing and subscription management:
- Go to Billing > Integrations > Stripe in your dashboard.
- Click "Connect Stripe" and follow the secure OAuth flow.
- Grant the necessary permissions for Skyymarkai to manage subscriptions and payments.
- Once connected, you can view and manage all transactions from your Stripe dashboard.

### Handling Payments and Subscriptions
All payments are processed securely via Stripe:
- Customers can pay using credit cards, ACH, or other supported methods.
- Subscriptions are managed automatically, with prorated charges for upgrades or downgrades.
- Failed payments trigger automated reminders and retry logic.

### Resolving Stripe Errors
Occasionally, payment issues may arise:
- Review error messages in the billing dashboard for details.
- Common issues include expired cards, insufficient funds, or authentication failures.
- Use the troubleshooting wizard to resolve common problems, or contact support for advanced help.

### Secure Payment Processing
Security is paramount:
- All payment data is encrypted and never stored on Skyymarkai servers.
- Stripe is PCI DSS compliant, ensuring industry-standard protection.
- Enable two-factor authentication on your Stripe account for added security.

### Stripe Dashboard Overview
Access your Stripe dashboard for a comprehensive view of all transactions, subscriptions, and payouts:
- Monitor revenue, refunds, and chargebacks in real time.
- Export financial reports for accounting and compliance.
- Set up alerts for unusual activity or large transactions.

### Common Issues and Solutions
Even the best systems encounter occasional issues. As a founder, you have access to advanced troubleshooting tools and direct support:
- **Login Problems:** Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link. If issues persist, contact support for account recovery.
- **Agent Errors:** Review agent logs for error messages. Restart or reconfigure agents as needed.
- **Billing Discrepancies:** Check your invoice history and payment methods. Contact support for billing adjustments.
- **Workflow Failures:** Use the troubleshooting wizard to diagnose and resolve workflow errors.

### Accessing Support Channels
Skyymarkai offers multiple support options for founders:
- **Live Chat:** Available directly in your dashboard for real-time assistance.
- **Email Support:** Reach out to support@skyymarkai.com for detailed inquiries.
- **Community Forum:** Connect with other founders to share solutions and best practices.

### Reporting Bugs and Feedback
Your feedback drives platform improvements:
- Use the "Report a Bug" feature in the dashboard to submit issues.
- Provide detailed steps to reproduce bugs for faster resolution.
- Suggest new features or enhancements via the feedback portal.

### System Status and Updates
Stay informed about platform health and new releases:
- Check the system status page for real-time updates on outages or maintenance.
- Subscribe to release notes for information on new features and bug fixes.

### Emergency Recovery Steps
In the event of a critical failure:

- Contact support immediately for high-priority incidents.
