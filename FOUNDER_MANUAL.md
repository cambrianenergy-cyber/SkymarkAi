## App Overview
Skyymarkai is a next-generation automation platform designed to streamline workflows, empower teams, and maximize business growth through AI-driven agents and orchestrator logic.

---

## Key Components

### 1. Agents
- **Purpose:** Agents automate specific business tasks (content creation, scheduling, analytics, outreach, etc.).
- **How They Work:** Each agent is a specialized module that receives instructions from the orchestrator and executes its assigned workflow.
- **Customization:** Agents can be added, removed, or upgraded based on your plan and business needs.

### 2. Orchestrator
- **Purpose:** The orchestrator coordinates all agent activities, manages workflow steps, and ensures seamless execution.
- **How It Works:** It receives high-level commands (e.g., run a campaign, generate reports), breaks them into steps, assigns agents, and tracks progress.
- **Observability:** All agent runs are logged for audit and troubleshooting.

### 3. Workflows & Templates
- **Purpose:** Workflows define multi-step processes (e.g., onboarding, marketing campaigns).
- **Templates:** Pre-built templates accelerate setup and ensure best practices.

### 4. Billing & Plans
- **Plans:** Foundation, Accelerate, Dominion, Sovereign. Each plan unlocks more agents, templates, and features.
- **Add-ons:** Premium agents and extra templates can be purchased as needed.

### 5. Security & Access
- **Founder Access:** Only the founder can access this manual and certain advanced controls.
- **Role Management:** Owners, admins, and members have different permissions.

---

## How Agents Perform Tasks
- Agents receive instructions from the orchestrator.
- Each agent runs its logic (e.g., content writing, analytics, reminders).
- Results are logged and can trigger further actions or notifications.
- Agents are modular and can be upgraded or replaced as business needs evolve.

---

## Orchestrator Command Flow
1. User initiates a workflow or task.
2. Orchestrator breaks the task into steps.
3. Each step is assigned to the appropriate agent.
4. Agent executes and returns results.
5. Orchestrator logs results, handles errors, and moves to the next step.
6. Completion triggers notifications or next actions.

## Troubleshooting & Observability
- All agent runs are logged in the Agent Activity dashboard.
- Errors and successes are tracked for each run.
- Founders can audit, debug, and optimize workflows from the dashboard.
