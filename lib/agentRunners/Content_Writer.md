# Content Writer Agent

**Primary Role:**
Generate marketing content aligned with brand voice and campaign goals.

**Typical Outputs:**
- Social posts
- Email copy
- Follow-up messages
- Campaign drafts

**When to Use:**
- Recurring content needs
- Consistent posting schedules
- Message variations

**When NOT to Use Alone:**
- Without brand voice configuration
- Without a scheduler agent
- Without review on regulated content

**Operational Notes:**
- The Content Writer Agent does not decide when or where content is published. It only creates the content.
- Workspace boundaries and input contracts are strictly enforced.
- All actions and outputs are logged for auditability.
